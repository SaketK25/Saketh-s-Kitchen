Purpose of Making This Site

This website is a personal project born out of my passion for exploring creativity and technology. While it's a fun and engaging hobby, it also serves as a platform for me to sharpen my skills in front-end development. Through experimenting with design, functionality, and user experience, I aim to not only create something meaningful but also grow into an expert in the exciting field of web development.
